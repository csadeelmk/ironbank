Philosophy


In A Song of Ice and Fire, the Iron Bank of Braavos is not run by a single person or group.


It's a complex institution with a hierarchy and a powerful network of key holders. These key holders hold keys to the bank's vaults and have a significant say in its operations.


When the Iron Bank invests in you we help you diversify your bet by participating in the fund as a key holder. You will hold participation in the Iron Bank fund. What we ask in return is that as a key holder you help your fellow founders build their empires. Its key holders help each other to build empires.
